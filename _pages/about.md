<p>My name is Sarun Nuntaviriyakul and  I'm an Associate Solution Engineer at <a href="https://www.vmware.com/">VMware</a>. I graduated from <a href="https://www.chula.ac.th/en/">Chulalongkorn University (CU)</a> major in Information and Communication Engineering (ICE).
Before joining VMware, I worked at <a href="https://www.sertiscorp.com/">Sertis</a> as a Machine Learning Engineer creating CI/CD pipelines to train, optimize, convert and deliver Deep Learning models to embedded-edge devices,  also developed a connector framework for multiple deep learning inference backend for minimizing the inference time of the deep learning model.
</p>

<p>
I'm in the Cloud-Native Application Team, focusing on Modern Applications, Cloud-Native, Containerized Platform, and CI/CD process.
One of my primary responsibility is conducting proof-of-concept and workshops to educate customers about containers, Kubernetes, and the DevSecOps process. I design and assist customers with application modernization, migrating from monolithic applications to containers and Kubernetes with the containerized platform on their virtual infrastructure.</p>
